===== MBA in Global Management =====
Our flagship MBA program equips future leaders with advanced business knowledge and international management skills. Students will master global strategy, cross-cultural communication, and contemporary business challenges while building a powerful network of business connections. This intensive program combines theoretical foundations with real-world applications through case studies, simulations, and interactions with industry leaders. Graduates are prepared for executive positions in multinational corporations, international consultancies, and global financial institutions.

===== BSc in Business Administration =====
The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration provides a comprehensive foundation in business fundamentals. Students develop essential skills in accounting, marketing, management, and finance while gaining practical experience through internships and case competitions. This program emphasizes analytical thinking, problem-solving, and effective communication – competencies highly valued in today's global marketplace. Graduates are prepared for diverse career paths in business or for continued studies at the graduate level.

===== MSc in International Business =====
The Master of Science in International Business delves deep into the complexities of global commerce and cross-border operations. Students explore international trade policies, global supply chain management, and multinational corporate strategy through an interdisciplinary lens. The program features specialized modules on emerging markets, international negotiations, and global marketing strategies. Graduates excel in roles requiring sophisticated understanding of international business environments and transnational operations.

===== PhD in Business Economics =====
Our doctoral program in Business Economics prepares scholars and researchers to advance knowledge at the intersection of economic theory and business practice. This rigorous program develops expertise in quantitative analysis, econometric methods, and advanced theoretical frameworks. Students conduct original research under faculty guidance, contributing to academic discourse on pressing economic issues affecting business and policy. Graduates pursue careers in academia, research institutions, and high-level economic analysis roles in government and private sectors.

===== Certificate in Digital Marketing =====
This specialized certificate program equips professionals with cutting-edge skills in the rapidly evolving digital marketing landscape. Students master search engine optimization, social media marketing, content strategy, and digital analytics through hands-on projects and real campaign development. The curriculum covers the latest digital platforms, consumer behavior trends, and data-driven marketing techniques. This program is ideal for marketing professionals seeking to enhance their digital capabilities or career-changers entering the marketing field.

===== Diploma in Project Management =====
The Project Management Diploma provides comprehensive training in planning, executing, and delivering successful projects across industries. Students learn essential methodologies including Agile, SCRUM, and traditional approaches while developing practical skills in risk assessment, budget management, and team leadership. The program aligns with PMI standards and prepares students for professional certification. Graduates are equipped to manage complex projects efficiently, delivering results on time and within budget constraints in any business environment.
